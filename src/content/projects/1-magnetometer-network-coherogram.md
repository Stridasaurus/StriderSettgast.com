A browser-based coherogram for ground-based magnetometer networks: it pulls
live solar-wind and magnetometer feeds, band-limits to the Pc3–Pc5 range
(2–50 mHz), and estimates windowed cross-coherence between station pairs to
surface coherent ULF waves against a statistical significance threshold.
